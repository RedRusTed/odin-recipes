Pepperoni pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 can Hunt's Tomato Sauce-No Salte added
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 prebaked thin pizza crust
  • 1 cup mozarella cheese
  • 1/3 cup sliced pepperoni

Step one

Preheat oven to 450Â°F. Combine tomato sauce, Parmesan cheese and oregano

in small bowl; spread tomato sauce evenly over crust. Sprinkle pizza with

mozzarella cheese; top with pepperoni.

Step two

Place on ungreased pizza pan. Bake 12 to 15 minutes or until edges of crust

are browned lightly and cheese melts. Cut pizza into 6 slices.
